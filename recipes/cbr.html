Chicken Broccoli Rice

Wonderful and healthy bowl of Chicken, Broccoli, and Rice.

This recipe will give the you absolute easiest and most boring meal prep there is!

It's the most basic whole-food sources of Protein, Carbs, and Veggies! Leaving you craving your next cheat meal after every bite!

Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs. of Chicken Breast
  • Honey
  • Sriracha
  • Garlic or Garlic Powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • 32 oz of Broccoli
  • 2 cups of White Rice
  • Chicken Bone Broth

Steps:

  1. Bake Chicken at 425 degrees for 17 minutes
  2. Put Broccoli in air fryer at 350 degrees for 7 minutes
  3. Put 2 cups White Rice in Rice Cooker, cook for preset time
  4. When all are finished cooking measure and divide up accordingly
